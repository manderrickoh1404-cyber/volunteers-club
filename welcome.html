About Us

Karatina University Volunteers Club is a student-led organization dedicated to serving local communities through charitable outreach, environmental activities, and educational support programs. We welcome students from all faculties who want to make a positive difference.

Our Vision

A compassionate society empowered by youth action.

Our Mission

Mobilize students to support vulnerable groups and promote sustainable development through hands-on projects.
